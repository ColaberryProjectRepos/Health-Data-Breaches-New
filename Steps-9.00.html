Importing the Data 9

Dataset: The data set includes recorded health data breaches in the U.S. from 2019 - 2021, compiled by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights.
 https://ocrportal.hhs.gov/ocr/breach/breach_report.jsf




1.     Click get data
2.     Choose Text/CSV
3.     Click File “breach_report.csv”
4.     Click box next to breach report
5.     Click Load



Get data from Comma Separated Value (.CSV) files
