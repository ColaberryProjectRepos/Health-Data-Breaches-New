Visualizations: Stacked Column and Line Charts 15

Breaches per Month by Type of Breach



  1. Add Stacked Column and Line Chart
  2. Drag Type of Breach to X-axis
  3. Drag BA Present and Breaches per Month measures to Column y-axis
  4. Drag Breaches YoY% and Affected YoY% to Line y-axis
  5. Format Visual and untoggled title for X-axis, Y-axis and Secondary y-axis
  6. Format General: Set height at 405 and Width at 655.  Set position at Horizontal 200 and Vertical 93
  7. Change colors to match cards: Format Visual, click dropdown for column change BA Present to #f0e199 and Breaches per Month #12239e
  8. Format Visual, click dropdown for lines, color and change Breaches YoY% to #6B007B and Affected YoY% to #E66C37
  9. Change Callout Value font color to match the chart. 
  10. In Format/Visual, click the dropdown for x-axis and untoggle “Title”
  11. In Format/Visual, click the dropdown for y-axis and untoggle “Title”
  12. Click the dropdown for Title.  In the title field type, “Breaches per Mth by Type of Breach”.  Change font color to ‘white’ and background color to ‘blue’.  Center the text.
