What is a KPI? How to Choose the Best KPI's for Your Business.


When choosing a dataset, you have to be certain that you could create at least 2 KPI's and the topic is interesting to make an engaging dashboard.  Once the dataset is chosen the KPI's are what tell the story.  To create an effective KPI to tell the story a total will not do.  There must be previous data to compare the current data to, showing a change over time to gain better insight as to what is happening.
